Instructions

Team Status Workflow:

  • Pending: New teams await admin approval
  • Open: Approved teams open for joining
  • Closed: Teams closed to new registrations
  • Withdrawn: Teams withdrawn from event
  • Cancelled: Permanently cancelled pending teams

Admin Actions:

  • Approve: Change pending teams to open status
  • Cancel: Permanently cancel pending teams
  • Withdraw: Mark teams as withdrawn
  • Delete: Permanently remove team and data
