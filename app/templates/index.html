What is Pantograph?

Pantograph is the registration and timing system for {{ config.EVENT_NAME }}. It lets participants:

  • Register teams
  • Join existing teams and share their preferences
  • Upload exchange photos for timing
  • Sign the participation waiver

Why does Pantograph use sign in with Google/GitHub/Microsoft?

Pantograph uses secure OAuth authentication to:

  • Verify participant identities for team registration
  • Ensure only team members can upload photos
  • Protect team data and prevent unauthorized access
  • Use your existing account instead of creating a new password
