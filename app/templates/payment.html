Baton Payment Information

What You're Getting

This $16 payment is for one official Light Rail Relay baton, which you'll receive on Angle Lake platform before the event. Each team is required to have a baton to participate in the relay. If you have a baton from a previous year, you do not need to purchase a new one. Bring it and run with it again!

Payment Instructions

To pay for your baton, please send $16 to @lightrailrelay on Venmo or PayPal. Please include your team name in the payment description.

Once your payment is received, your team captain will be notified and your team's registration will be approved.

Refund Policy

Payments are refundable until midnight September 13th, 2025. Simply contact us. Beyond this point, consider inviting someone else to be captain of the team to use your baton if you can't participate.

If the event is cancelled or rescheduled, any team may request a refund.
