Baton Payment Information

What You're Getting

This $16 payment is for one official Light Rail Relay baton, which you'll receive on the Angle Lake platform before the event. Each team is required to have a baton to participate in the relay. If you have a baton from a previous year, you do not need to purchase a new one. Bring it and run with it again!

Payment Instructions

To pay for your baton, please send $16 to nick@nickwalker.us on Venmo or PayPal. Please include "LRR" and your team name in the payment description.

Once your payment is processed, your team captain will be notified and your team's registration will be approved.

Refund Policy

Payments are refundable until midnight September 13th, 2025. Contact us for a refund. Beyond this point, consider inviting someone else to be captain of the team to use your baton if you can't participate. There are no refunds if you fail to pick up your baton by the scheduled start of the event.

If the event is cancelled or rescheduled, all teams may request a refund or make an alternate arrangement to receive their baton.
